Not sure what to say, other than the fact this was one of the nicest meals I've had in months.


Slightly set aside from the lively and rowdy Restaurant Row, this is a classy restaurant with a matching menu and wine list that befits its surroundings. Put it this way, I felt I was an adult having a nice meal and it felt special.


The chicken pot pie was excellent, a thick creamy sauce and sweet peas. The steak was juicy and tender, but the best bit was the desserts. A pie homemade by the daughter of the owners and a bread N' butter pudding laced with sweetness throughout. If the savory wasn't as good I would have just eaten pudding for three courses!


The owners are truly invested in their new operation, both being visible front of house and back. I love the homemade desserts too - so many good JC restaurants seem to forget about or fall short when it comes to the final course.


So, all in all, a really welcome addition to JC. Don't tell too many people as it'll get too busy
